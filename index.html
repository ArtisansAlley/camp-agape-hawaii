Agape Christian Fellowship Center

Agape Christian fellowship Center uses faith and action to implement a vision for a large-scale community center in Mililani that can foster emotional, spiritual, and educational growth for courageous individuals young and old who desire transformation for themselves, their families, and the community. Agape Fellowship

We are a vibrant church with a congregation that looks to experience God in an intimate way. We partner with our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ to express the joy and power of His Kingdom in every area of life. We look to empower those whose families have been affected by parents who have been incarcerated and hope to strengthen their relationship with God.

Camp Agape

Camp Agape is an annual, four-day event filled with an intentional series of activities and events that facilitate sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ through love, trust, forgiveness, and hope. Camp Agape is the place where astonishing transformations happen that affect the whole family and can last generations. The camp is just the beginning of a transformed life with Christ. With the new center, we will be able to provide more activities and events throughout the year that keep the children connected with their mentors, who love and encourage them.

“And are built upon the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Jesus Christ himself being the chief corner.”-Ephesians 2:20-22

Prison Outreach

Agape prison Outreach conducts regular outreaches to the men and women incarcerated in Oahu’s prisons and correctional facilities. The ministry also makes annual trips to Saguaro Correctional Center in Eloy, Arizona, a mainland prison that houses inmates from the state of Hawaii.

Through Bible study, worship and prayer, inmates are given a chance to break free from mental, emotional, and spiritual bondage, and are encouraged and empowered to become productive members of society when they’re released from prison.
