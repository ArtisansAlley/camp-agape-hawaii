Center for Transformation

Why a Center?

This center will be for the educational growth for courageous individuals young and old who desire transformation for themselves, their families, and the community. This faculty will encompass these three main ministries. Camp Agape, Prison Ministry and Agape Christian Fellowship. We envision this central facility to be used year-round for youth programs, team-building and leadership training that will benefit children and youth as well as adults from all across the Islands.

A Vision of the past, present, and future

What We Need

We will need to raise a total of $25 Million for the Construction of this all purpose camp. An additional $5.0 million to be invested into a permanent endowment fund to provide for a major portion of the annual operating costs. The initial vision for this facility was due to the lack of available facilities and because of the scarcity of available venues and the astronomical cost of available facilities, our island is in dire need of a facility that will increase the moral compass of our children and youth and train them to become leaders in our community, state and world. The parents of these children who were incarcerated also will participate in program at this center. The new state of the art facilities will be available to all. Plans call for partnering with local organizations, schools, churches, youth groups and many other local businesses to ensure maximum utilization of the facilities where individuals will grow in their social, psychological, physical and life skills.

While this construction project is an ambitious one, Pastor Roy calls it the Super Bowl of Kingdom work. Our programs will teach important life skills from cooking healthy meals on a budget, to self-sustainability through such ministries such as agriculture and aquaponics to learn self sustainability to name a few projected programs.
